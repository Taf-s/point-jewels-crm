## Getting Started

**Accessing the System:**
1. Open your browser and go to the admin link your developer provides
2. You'll see the main dashboard with a sidebar on the left
3. You'll see three main sections:
   - **Jewelry Piece** - Your products
   - **Collection** - Groups of related jewelry
   - **Team Member** - People who have access

---

## Adding Your First Product

### Step 1: Create a New Product
1. Click **"Jewelry Piece"** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **"Create"** button (usually at the top)
3. A form will appear

### Step 2: Fill in Basic Information
- **Name of Piece** - What do you call it? (e.g., "Delicate Heart Necklace")
- **Slug** - This auto-fills from the name (don't worry about it for now)
- **Category** - Choose one:
  - Necklace
  - Ring
  - Bracelet
  - Pendant
  - Watch
- **Gallery** - Upload your beautiful photos! 📸
  - Click to upload or drag & drop
  - Add at least one photo (more is better!)
- **Romance Copy** - Tell the story! Write what makes this piece special
  - This is where you write your poetic description
  - Example: *"Handcrafted with love, this delicate necklace captures the essence of timeless elegance..."*
- **Collection** (Optional) - Is this part of a special collection? Skip for now if not

### Step 3: Add Materials & Pricing
This is the powerful part! You can offer the SAME design in different materials.

1. Scroll to **"Materials & Pricing"**
2. Click **"Add item"**
3. A new variant row appears. Fill in:
   - **Material** - Choose (18k Gold, Rose Gold, Platinum, Sterling Silver)
   - **Price (ZAR)** - What does this version cost?
   - **Stock Quantity** - How many do you have in stock?
   - **SKU** - Create a unique code for tracking
     - Format: TYPE-MATERIAL-NUMBER
     - Example: `NECKL-HEART-GOLD` or `RING-GOLD-001`

4. Click **"Add item"** again if you want to add another variant (e.g., silver version)

### Step 4: Save
- Click the **"Publish"** or **"Save"** button at the top right
- Your product is now in the system! ✅

## Managing Variants (Gold & Silver)

### What Are Variants?
Instead of creating two separate products for "Gold Ring" and "Silver Ring," you create ONE product with TWO variants. This keeps everything organized.

### Example: Creating a Ring with Two Materials

**Product Name:** Classic Engagement Ring

**Variant 1:**
- Material: 18k Gold
- Price: R5,999
- Stock: 3 pieces
- SKU: RING-CLASS-GOLD

**Variant 2:**
- Material: Sterling Silver
- Price: R1,999
- Stock: 7 pieces
- SKU: RING-CLASS-SILVER

Same beautiful ring, two price points, separate stock tracking. Perfect!

### Updating Stock
When you sell a piece:
1. Find the product
2. Click on it
3. Find the variant (gold or silver)
4. Update the "Stock Quantity" number
5. Save

## Creating Collections

Collections help you group related products. Great for marketing campaigns or seasonal releases.

### Step 1: Create a Collection
1. Click **"Collection"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Create"**

### Step 2: Fill in Collection Details
- **Collection Name** - What do you call this group?
  - Examples: "Engagement Rings", "Summer 2025", "Bestsellers"
- **Slug** - Auto-fills (don't worry)
- **Collection Description** - Tell the story of this collection
  - Example: *"These are our most romantic engagement rings, perfect for the moment that matters most..."*
- **Collection Image** - Upload one hero photo that represents the whole collection

### Step 3: Link Products to This Collection
1. Go back to your products
2. Edit a product
3. In the **Collection** field, select the collection you just created
4. Save

**Tip:** You can add the same product to multiple collections if it fits!

## Managing Your Team

This section is for you to invite people to help manage your business.

### Adding a Team Member
1. Click **"Team Member"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Create"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Full Name**
   - **Email Address**
   - **Role** - Choose one:
     - **Owner/Master** (that's you!) - Can do everything
     - **Product Manager** - Can add and edit products
     - **Viewer** - Can only see products (useful for previews)
   - **Active** - Toggle ON (means they have access)

### Removing Access
If you want to remove someone's access:
1. Find the team member
2. Toggle **Active** to OFF
3. Save

This deactivates them without deleting their record. You can reactivate later if needed.

## Tips & Tricks

### 📸 Photography Tips
- Take photos on a clean, bright background
- Show the jewelry from multiple angles
- Include close-ups of details
- Upload your best photo first (it shows as the thumbnail)

### 💰 Pricing Strategy
- Remember: Gold costs more than silver
- Consider your material costs + your craft value
- Don't undervalue your work!

### 🏷️ SKU Best Practices
- Keep SKUs simple and logical
- Use your category as the first part: `NECKL-`, `RING-`, `BRACE-`, `PEND-`, `WATCH-`
- Then the material: `GOLD`, `SILVER`, `PLAT`
- Then a number: `001`, `002`, etc.
- Example: `NECKL-GOLD-001`

### 🎨 Romance Copy Ideas
Your "Romance Copy" is where you shine! Don't just describe—tell a story:
- ❌ "Silver necklace with heart pendant"
- ✅ "A whisper of elegance. This delicate silver necklace, adorned with a handcrafted heart pendant, is perfect for moments of pure connection..."

### 📱 Before You Publish
Always check:
- ✅ At least one beautiful photo
- ✅ All variants have prices and stock
- ✅ SKUs are unique
- ✅ Romance copy tells your story
